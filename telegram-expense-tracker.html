Featured Project

Telegram Bot for Personal Finance Tracking

Log your expenses via Telegram – stored securely with AWS and Google Sheets

Overview

This Telegram bot simplifies personal expense tracking by allowing users to log expenses through chat messages. By leveraging AWS Lambda and Google Sheets, it provides a seamless experience for maintaining financial records without the need for complex spreadsheets or apps.

Why It Matters

Managing personal finances shouldn't be a chore. This bot transforms the way you track expenses by making it as simple as sending a message. No more forgetting to log expenses or dealing with complicated apps – just quick, efficient financial tracking that fits into your daily routine.

How It Works

Send expense data via Telegram chat

Google Sheets
